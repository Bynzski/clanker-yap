# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

Contributors are expected to keep discussion respectful, direct, and constructive.

Examples of expected behavior:

- assume good intent
- focus feedback on the code and the behavior, not the person
- be specific about bugs, tradeoffs, and proposed fixes
- accept technical disagreement without hostility

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- harassment or personal attacks
- discriminatory or sexualized language
- trolling, intimidation, or sustained disruption
- publishing private information without permission

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove comments, issues, pull requests, or contributors
that violate this standard.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, open a private channel with
the maintainers rather than escalating publicly.
